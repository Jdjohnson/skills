# Intake

Capture only what matters:

- goal: what the piece needs to accomplish
- audience: who will read it
- format: post, article, memo, email draft, note, script, or other
- source: pasted notes, files, links, calls, meetings, or prior drafts
- constraints: length, tone, claims to include, claims to avoid, deadline

If the user gives a brain dump, summarize the intended point in plain language and ask whether that is the right direction before drafting.

If facts are missing, mark them as assumptions or questions. Do not invent details.
