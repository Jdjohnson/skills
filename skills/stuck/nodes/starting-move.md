## Starting Move

1. Translate the big goal into one observable action.
2. Prefer a move small enough to begin in one sitting.
3. If the move is still abstract, shrink it.
4. Phrase it as a real action, not an intention.
5. Optimize for traction, not elegance.

Good forms:

- open the draft and write three rough bullets
- send one clarifying question
- make the list of missing inputs
- spend ten minutes on the hardest part first

Avoid finish-line outcomes like "solve the whole thing" or "get organized."
