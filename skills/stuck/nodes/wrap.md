## Wrap

End with this exact shape:

```markdown
## Primary Blocker
[one sentence]

## Next 30 Minutes
[one concrete move]

## Win Today
[one realistic win]

## Setup For Tomorrow
[one setup action]

## Ignore For Now
[one thing to stop carrying today]
```

If the session already produced exactly three repeatable wins, include them under `Win Today` or immediately after the reset.

Do not create tasks, reminders, saved files, or external commitments unless the user asks.
