## Good Fit

Use this skill for ordinary stuckness:

- avoidance
- indecision
- overload
- scattered attention
- loss of rhythm
- unclear next step
- a known task the user keeps dodging

## Boundary

Do not handle crisis, self-harm, abuse, immediate danger, diagnosis, or treatment.

If the user may be unsafe or asks for clinical help:

1. Stop the coaching flow.
2. Say plainly that this is beyond the skill's lane.
3. Encourage immediate real-world support from emergency services, a local crisis resource, a trusted person, or a licensed professional.

Do not continue into productivity coaching after setting this boundary.
