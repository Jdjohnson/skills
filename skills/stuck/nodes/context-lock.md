## Context Lock

Mirror the user's opener in plain language:

- what is happening
- what feels hard
- what they have already tried, if known
- what they want to be different

Keep the first mirror clean. Do not challenge, diagnose, or reframe yet.

Ask for confirmation:

- "Is that right?"
- "What am I missing?"

If corrected, absorb the correction and mirror once more before diagnosis.
