## Diagnosis

Ask one short question at a time until one primary thread is clear.

Common blockers:

- avoidance: something uncomfortable is being dodged
- fog: the next step is unclear
- overload: too many open loops are competing
- confidence: the user does not trust the next move
- energy: the plan is too large for the current day
- interference: a habit or environment keeps pulling attention away

Name the best diagnosis plainly and ask the user to confirm or correct it.

Keep secondary tensions to one short note. Do not let the plan sprawl.

## Branch Choice

- Avoidance is primary: use [[avoidance.md]], then [[starting-move.md]].
- Fog or overload is primary: use [[starting-move.md]], then [[three-wins.md]].
- Interference is primary: use [[interfering-habit.md]], then [[three-wins.md]].
- Confidence or energy is primary: shrink the move with [[starting-move.md]], then wrap.
