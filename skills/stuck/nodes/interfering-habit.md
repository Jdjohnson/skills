## Interfering Habit

1. Ask what pattern keeps breaking the reset.
2. Offer examples only as prompts:
   - reactive inbox
   - endless planning
   - perfectionist prep
   - avoidance scrolling
   - overcommitting
   - checking out
3. Choose one primary habit or loop to interrupt.
4. Define one interruption rule, environmental change, or replacement move.
5. Keep it practical and non-moralizing.

## Output

- one primary interfering habit
- one clear way to interrupt it
