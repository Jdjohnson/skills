## Avoidance

1. Ask what the user least wants to touch right now.
2. Separate the concrete avoided thing from the fear, shame, or story around it.
3. Keep shrinking until the avoided thing can be named in one plain sentence.
4. When useful, ask what avoiding it is costing.
5. Do not moralize. The point is honesty, not shame.

## Output

- one clearly named avoided thing
- enough surrounding truth to make the next move honest
