## Intake Lock

Do not dispatch the panel until the user confirms or corrects the framing.

Restate:

- locked question

- audience

Ask the smallest question needed to improve the lock.

## Good Fit

Use this for questions about:

- AI and labor
- productivity and growth
- inequality and distribution
- firm adaptation
- regional resilience
- technological change
- policy, measurement, or institutions

If the topic lacks economic shape, clarify the question before dispatch.
