## Preconditions

Only dispatch after the intake lock has:

- question
- intended output
- audience
- depth
- approved panel

## Per-Economist Extraction

For each approved economist, extract:

- standing position on the locked topic
- likely agreement
- likely objection
- evidence they would want
- question they would ask back
- sourced claims vs extrapolated claims

Use the bundled reference cache first. Use [[source-discipline.md]] for freshness rules.

## Synthesis

Do not paste mini-essays back-to-back. Synthesize across the panel:

- where they converge
- where they diverge
- strongest pushback
- practical implications
- what remains uncertain
- one useful question back to the user

Use [[output-format.md]] for the final shape.
