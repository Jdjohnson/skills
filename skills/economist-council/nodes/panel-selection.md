## Default Panel

Default to the Big Five unless the user asks for a narrower or broader panel:

- David Autor
- Andrew McAfee
- Erik Brynjolfsson
- Daron Acemoglu
- Philippe Aghion

## Optional Bench

Add bench economists only when their frame materially helps:

- Carl Benedikt Frey: historical parallels, displacement timing, technology traps
- Anton Korinek: transformative AI, regime shifts, policy under rapid change
- Pascual Restrepo: task decomposition, labor share, automation bottlenecks
- Diane Coyle: measurement, welfare, GDP, public value
- Michael Webb: occupational exposure and high-skill risk
- Jason Furman: policy realism, regulation, macro tradeoffs
- Anna Salomons: labor-market adaptation, training, Europe
- Joel Mokyr: long-run history, institutions, useful knowledge

When adding bench voices, give a one-line rationale and confirm the panel before dispatch.
