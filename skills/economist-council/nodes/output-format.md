## Dispatch Output

```markdown
## Question
[locked question]

## Bottom Line
[plain-language synthesis]

## Convergence
- ...

## Divergence
- ...

## Strongest Pushback
- ...

## Practical Implications
- ...

## Uncertainty And Source Notes
- [sourced, extrapolated, or unverified]

## One Question Back
[best next question for the user]
```

## Writing Rules

- Keep the council as synthesis, not a stack of separate biographies.
- Name confidence in plain language.
- Do not overstate the panel's certainty.
- Make disagreement useful for a decision, talk, article, or strategy move.
