## Quick Commands

- `skip`: make a reasonable assumption, label it, and continue.
- `you decide`: choose the strongest default, label it, and continue.
- `go deeper on [x]`: run the next round on that angle.
- `push harder`: increase specificity and adversarial pressure.
- `devil's advocate`: spend the next round on the strongest opposing case.
- `what am I missing`: surface blind spots and unresolved risks.
- `steelman the other side`: produce the best charitable counterargument before proceeding.
- `wrap it up`: route to [[wrap-synthesis.md]].

Do not reset the interview because of a quick command. Treat it as a steering input inside the same thread.
