## Default

Return the final brief in chat. Save a file only when the user asks or provides a destination.

## Brief Template

```markdown
# [Title] - Steelman Brief

## The Idea
[1-3 sentence statement of what this is]

## Why It Matters
[real motivation, including stated and underlying reasons]

## The Strongest Case For
[best argument in favor, written for a skeptic]

## The Strongest Case Against
[strongest charitable counterargument]

## Key Assumptions
1. [tested | untested | risky] ...

## Tradeoffs
[costs, opportunity costs, and who or what loses]

## Open Questions
[unresolved risks or landmines]

## What Success Looks Like
[specific observable outcomes]

## First Steps
[concrete next actions]

## Interview Notes
[important quotes, tensions, assumptions, and decisions]
```

## Adaptation

- Arguments: make `The Strongest Case Against` sharper.
- Personal decisions: emphasize `Tradeoffs` and `Open Questions`.
- Business strategies: emphasize `Key Assumptions` and `First Steps`.
- Creative projects: emphasize `Why It Matters` and audience fit.
