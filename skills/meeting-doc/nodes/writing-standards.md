description: Evidence and writing standards for meeting documents.
---

## Standards

- Lead with what matters.
- Keep routine meetings short.
- Do not pad with fake strategy.
- Separate decisions from suggestions.
- Separate commitments from possible follow-up.
- Say when the record is too thin.
- Use bullets when they improve scan speed.
- Preserve uncertainty instead of inventing owners, deadlines, or decisions.

## Evidence

Ground every talking point, decision, commitment, and watchout in the supplied context.

If a claim is a reasonable inference, label it as an inference.

If the user wants a follow-up message, draft it separately and do not send it unless the host and user explicitly support sending.
