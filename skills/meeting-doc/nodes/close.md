description: Meeting closeout format and behavior.

## Close

Use this when the user wants to capture what happened after a meeting.

Prefer actual notes, transcripts, or the user's debrief. If the record is thin, ask a short debrief:

- What actually mattered?
- Any decision or commitment worth capturing?
- What should happen next, and by whom?

## Output

```markdown
# Meeting Closeout: [Meeting Title]

## What Happened
[concise recap]

## Decisions
- ...

## Commitments
- ...

## Open Questions
- ...

## Suggested Follow-Up
- ...
```

If no decisions or commitments were captured, say so plainly. Do not create tasks or send follow-ups unless the user asks.
