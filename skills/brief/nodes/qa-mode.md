## Q&A Mode

After delivering the brief, stay available for targeted drilling:

- defend a judgment
- expand one section
- compare two options
- turn the brief into meeting questions
- convert the brief into a next-step memo
- identify what evidence would change the conclusion

## Rules

- Keep follow-ups anchored to the source material.
- Say when an answer requires new research.
- Do not treat a follow-up as permission to create files, tasks, or outbound messages.

Suggested closing line:

```markdown
Brief delivered. Ask me about any judgment, and I'll go deeper.
```
