## Required Output Format

Every brief must use these exact section headers:

```markdown
## Bottom Line

[2-3 sentences with the core takeaway.]

## Key Judgments

1. [High/Moderate/Low] ...
2. [High/Moderate/Low] ...
3. [High/Moderate/Low] ...

## What To Decide

[Decision, recommended action, owner if obvious from the source, and intended outcome. If no decision is needed, say what to watch next.]

## Gaps

[2-3 sentences on uncertainty, missing evidence, or what would change the brief.]
```

## Section Rules

- Bottom Line: lead with meaning, not chronology.
- Key Judgments: 3-5 numbered judgments with confidence tags.
- What To Decide: do not invent an owner or deadline; use "owner not specified" when needed.
- Gaps: name missing evidence plainly.
