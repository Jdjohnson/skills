## Calibration

Load [[source-of-truth.md]] first.

Infer rules from the available evidence. Focus on patterns that affect writing choices:

- sentence length and rhythm
- vocabulary and formality
- warmth, humor, or restraint
- paragraph shape
- directness and certainty
- how claims are supported
- transitions and structure
- words, moves, or tones to avoid

Turn observations into operational rules. Do not write labels like "authentic," "bold," or "professional" unless you explain what that changes in the copy.

## Missing Evidence

If no examples are available, ask for one of these:

- 2-3 writing samples
- a style guide
- a draft that feels close
- a draft that feels wrong
- a short description of the intended audience and channel

If the user asks you to proceed anyway, create a provisional profile and clearly mark the assumptions.
