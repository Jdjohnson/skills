## Phase 6: Draft

Write in a clear human voice:

- specific over generic
- concrete over abstract
- direct over ornate
- varied sentence length
- no filler transitions
- no invented facts or stories
- no fake certainty

Honor the target channel. A memo, article, email, social post, and talk should not have the same rhythm.

## If Asked To "Just Draft It"

Make reasonable assumptions from the prompt. Do not pause unless the missing information would make the draft misleading or useless.

## Output

```markdown
## Draft

[draft]

## Assumptions / Remaining Choices
- ...
```

Keep the assumptions short. The draft is the work.
