## Phase 2: Goal

Clarify what the reader should:

- think
- feel
- do

The goal should be specific enough to guide structure and ending. Avoid vague goals like "inform people" unless the piece is truly informational.

## Output

```markdown
## Reader Goal

- Think:
- Feel:
- Do:
```

If the user does not know, propose a reasonable goal and mark it as an assumption.
