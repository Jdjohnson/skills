## Phase 4: Research

Research only when facts, dates, claims, comparisons, current context, or examples matter.

Use proportional effort:

- No research for personal reflection, supplied source material, or voice-only cleanup.
- Light research for a supporting fact, example, or quote.
- Deeper research only when the piece depends on contested claims or current information.

When browsing or search is unavailable, say what cannot be verified and proceed with caveats if useful.

## Rules

- Track which claims are sourced.
- Do not invent citations.
- Do not overbuild a research report when the user asked for prose.
- If a claim cannot be verified, either soften it or mark it for confirmation.

## Output

```markdown
## Research Notes

- Confirmed:
- Needs confirmation:
- Do not claim yet:
```
