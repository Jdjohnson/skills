# Research Detour

Use a research detour only when a factual gap is blocking better thinking.

Before researching, state:

- the question to answer
- why it matters
- how deep the detour should be

Keep detours short. Bring the result back into the brainstorm as options, constraints, or changed assumptions.

Save source material only when the user asks or when `ROUTES.md` requires it for work that will continue.
